Assess & build your Entrepreneurial IQ

We beleive that entrepreneurial skills, knowledge and attitudes can be learned and in turn lead to the widespread development of entrepreneurial mind-sets and culture, which benefit individuals and society as a whole.
The competence framework identifies 3 interrelated and interconnected competence areas:
  • Ideas & Opportunities
  • Resources
  • Intro Action
that directly mirror the definition of entrepreneurship as the ability to turn ideas into action that generate value for someone other than oneself
