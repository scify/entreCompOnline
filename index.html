We believe that entrepreneurial skills, knowledge and attitudes can be learned and in turn lead to the widespread development of entrepreneurial mind-sets and culture, which benefit individuals and society as a whole.

The Entrepreneuship competence framework

The competence framework identifies 3 interrelated and interconnected competence areas: a)Ideas & Opportunities b)Resources c) Intro Action that directly mirror the definition of entrepreneurship as the ability to turn ideas into action that generate value for someone other than oneself
